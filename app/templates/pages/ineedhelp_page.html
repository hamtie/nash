Is this an emergency?

If you are thinking of hurting yourself or someone else, please don't do that. Call 911 or the emergency services where you live. We know this is really scary! We promise that this is the right thing to do.

Hugs

So you think you might need help? First off, so many hugs. Some of us have been where you are, and it's not a fun place to be. It takes a lot of courage to tell anyone that you are having trouble, and you should be proud of yourself for having that courage.

Find someone you can trust

We recommend finding one or two people who can help you. Think about the people you know - who would you trust the most to listen to you and not judge you? Send them a Nash invite from the Friends page.

Make a list of what's troubling you

What things are you not sure about? What things do you currently think are true that you don't think most people would agree with? Make a list of these things and then go to the New Graph page. We will help you turn this list into a diagam that explains to people what's going on with you, and lets them help you figure out what's going on.

Remember that everyone has a point of view

It's likely that you've just had a lot of weird experiences that you can't explain, but remember that other people may not have been there with you for those experiences, or they may have a different view of what those experiences mean.

Remember that everyone who counts is rooting for you!

We're rooting for you too.

Let's get started!

Invite a friend on the Friends page, then head to the New Graph page.
