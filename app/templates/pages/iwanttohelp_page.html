Is this an emergency?

If you think your friend is thinking of hurting themselves or someone else, please call 911 or the emergency services where you live. We know this is really scary! We promise that this is the right thing to do.

BUT, please don't do this just because your friend believes something weird. Weird and dangerous is dangerous, weird and not dangerous is not dangerous.

Hugs

So you think your friend might need help? First off, so many hugs. Some of us have been where you are, and it's not a fun place to be. Thanks for being there for your friend!

Please keep this confidential

Your friend is having trouble telling what is real and what is not, and our society really stigmatizes anything that looks remotely like that kind of trouble. People can and do lose jobs and relationships because of that stigma. If your friend has shared anything with you on this site, they've put a huge amount of trust in you, and it's up to you not to betray that trust.

Remember that everyone has a point of view

It's likely that your friend has just had a lot of weird experiences that they can't explain. They may believe things that you have a hard time discussing calmly. If you want to help your friend, it is best if you can stay calm and tell them which things make sense to you and which things do not make sense to you, in a patient and non-judgmental way. It will help immensely if you can acknowledge that you cannot be certain of things that happened when you were not with your friend.

Please do not lie to your friend or humor them

Your friend is in a very vulnerable place right now. Please try to treat them as you would want to be treated if you were in their shoes - straightforwardly, and with respect and dignity.

Remember that everyone who counts is rooting for your friend!

We're rooting for them too.
